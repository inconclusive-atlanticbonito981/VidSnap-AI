## 🛠️ Making your first video

Creating a video involves four simple phases. 

### 1. Project details
Start by selecting the New Project button. Name your project and choose a folder on your computer to save the resulting files. Select the aspect ratio for your video, such as vertical for mobile platforms or horizontal for standard screens.

### 2. Add your text
Paste your script into the text area. The application breaks your text into segments based on sentences. You can select different voices for each segment to give your video a professional tone. Use the preview button to listen to the audio before you proceed.

### 3. Import your images
Drag and drop your images into the project window. You can change the order of your images to match your narration. The software displays a timeline where you can set how long each image stays on screen. Syncing your images with the audio track helps maintain viewer interest.

### 4. Create the file
Once you are happy with the preview, click the Generate Video button. The computer processes your images and audio to create a single video file. This process may take a few minutes depending on the length of your video and the speed of your processor. The software notifies you when the rendering finishes.

## ⚙️ Understanding the main tools

The user interface keeps controls simple. Here is a breakdown of what you see on the screen.

- **The Canvas:** This is the preview area. You see your images and the layout of the current scene here.
- **The Timeline:** This bar shows your images in sequence. You can click and drag items to reorganize your video flow.
- **The Voice Panel:** This allows you to pick from various voice profiles. You can adjust the stability and clarity settings for each voice.
- **The Export Button:** This saves your final work to a video file. Choose the format that fits your needs, such as MP4, which works on almost every social media site.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

### Can I use my own music?
Yes. You can import background music files in the project settings menu. The software allows you to adjust the volume levels of the narrator and the background music independently.

### Does the software work without the internet?
You need an internet connection to generate the voice narration. The voice service lives on external servers. After the audio generates, you can finish your video edits and export the final file while working offline.

### Where can I find my finished videos?
The software saves your files in the folder you selected during the project setup phase. You can check your project settings at any time to see the exact location on your hard drive.

### The preview video lags. Is something broken?
High-quality previews use significant amounts of system memory. If the preview jitters, close other open programs to free up computer power. This does not affect the quality of the final exported video.

### How do I update to the latest version?
Download the newest installer from the link shown at the top of this page. Run the installer again. The program detects the old version and replaces it with the updated one while keeping your settings.

### What files does the program support?
You can use common image formats like JPG, PNG, and BMP. The video export happens in the MP4 format, which is the industry standard for online sharing.
